Cycler

Jump to an app. Press again to walk its windows.

Bind a hotkey to an app once. The first press brings it to the front; press the same key again to step through that app's open windows, one at a time. No Dock hunting, no Mission Control. Lives in the menu bar, stays out of the way.

In development. Follow along on GitHub →

How it works

  1. Bind a key to an app — for example, Hyper + 1 → Chrome.
  2. Press it — Chrome comes to the front.
  3. Press it again — Cycler walks to Chrome's next window, then the next, wrapping around.

A Caiano utility for macOS 13+.
